WHY IGLOO?

$IGLOO is not just another meme token. It is a fully decentralised cold-chain protocol built on Solana with a deflationary supply mechanism, on-chain governance, and real yield distribution to stakers.

Every transaction burns 0.5% of supply. Every week, 60% of protocol revenue is distributed to $IGLOO stakers. The remaining 40% fuels the treasury.
